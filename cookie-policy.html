Connect Digitals - Understanding Our Use of Cookies

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and understand how you use our site.

Cookie Notice

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submissions.

  • Session management and security
  • Form validation and processing
  • Language and region preferences
  • Cookie consent management

2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and choices.

  • User interface customization
  • Remembering your language preference
  • Storing form data temporarily
  • Enabling interactive features

2.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Time spent on pages
  • Traffic sources and referrals
  • Device and browser information

2.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements.

  • Social media integration
  • Advertising campaign tracking
  • Remarketing and retargeting
  • Social sharing functionality

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages:

4.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your use of our site.

  • Pages you visit and time spent
  • How you reached our website
  • Your device and browser information
  • Demographic information (if available)

4.2 Social Media

Social media platforms may place cookies when you interact with social sharing buttons or embedded content.

  • Facebook (for sharing and tracking)
  • Instagram (for embedded content)
  • TikTok (for embedded content)
  • Telegram (for sharing functionality)

4.3 Formspree

Our contact form uses Formspree for processing. They may place cookies to ensure proper form functionality.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

5.2 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences
  • Change your settings later

5.3 Opt-Out Options

For specific third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Facebook: Facebook Ad Preferences
  • General: Your Online Choices

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features of our website may not work properly:

  • Forms may not function correctly
  • Your preferences won't be remembered
  • Some interactive features may be limited
  • Analytics data may be incomplete

Essential Cookies

We recommend keeping essential cookies enabled for the best experience. These cookies are necessary for basic website functionality.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect:

  • Changes in our cookie usage
  • New third-party services
  • Legal requirements
  • Technology improvements

We will notify you of significant changes through our website or email.
